How It Works

Our intelligent intake system handles every call professionally, capturing the information you need to evaluate and respond to new cases.

1

Caller Speaks with Intelligent Assistant

When someone calls your firm, they’re greeted by a professional assistant that can handle complex conversations naturally.

2

Key Case Details Are Captured

The system automatically extracts and organizes critical information: contact details, case type, incident date, injuries, opposing parties, and urgency level.

3

Consultation Scheduled or Message Routed

Based on your preferences, the system either books a consultation directly or routes urgent matters to you immediately.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Legal Client Intake.

How long does setup take?

Typically within days depending on your intake questions, routing preferences, and the practice areas you want covered first.

Can I customize the intake questions?

Yes. Intake workflows can be tailored by practice area so you capture the exact information you need before calling the lead back.

Can calls be transferred to an attorney?

Yes. You can define “urgent” criteria and route those calls immediately while standard leads are captured and summarized.

How does it handle urgent or emotional callers?

The intake flow stays calm and structured, focusing on essentials and de-escalation while capturing key details and urgency.
